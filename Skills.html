Skills

This is what I do.

Over the years, I have developed a diverse set of skills that I am passionate about. These skills span across various areas, from practical abilities like cooking and playing the guitar to more athletic pursuits like soccer. I am always looking to improve and learn new things, and I take pride in the progress I've made so far. Below are some of the key skills I've honed through practice and dedication.

While I enjoy exploring a wide range of activities, I believe that developing expertise in a few areas allows me to build strong foundations and use those skills in multiple settings. Whether it's creating delicious meals, playing music to share with others, or excelling in sports, I am committed to improving my talents and utilizing them to their fullest potential. Each of these activities also teaches me the importance of patience, perseverance, and self-discipline, all of which are valuable in both my personal and professional life.

I strongly believe that life is a constant learning experience, and I'm always open to discovering new hobbies and talents. Each of these skills contributes to my well-rounded personality and is something I enjoy sharing with others. As I continue my education and career journey, I aim to take the lessons learned from each of these activities and apply them to my work and future endeavors.

  1. Cooking
    • Specializing in Mexican cuisine, I have learned to prepare flavorful dishes like tacos, enchiladas, and tamales.
    • As a fan of Chilean food, I have mastered recipes like pastel de choclo and empanadas, bringing a taste of South America to the kitchen.
    • In addition to these, I enjoy cooking smoked southern dishes, including ribs, brisket, and pulled pork, using traditional techniques and flavors.
  2. Guitar
    • I can comfortably play 6 hymns, allowing me to contribute to church services and gatherings by providing live music. I also enjoy learning new songs to expand my repertoire.
    • I've developed a keen ear for music and am working on improving my skills to play more complex pieces, particularly within acoustic and classical guitar genres.
  3. Soccer
    • I have experience playing every position on the left side of the field, from left-back to left wing, and have gained a thorough understanding of the strategies for each role.
    • In my youth, I was proud to achieve the fastest kick in Idaho for my age group, which highlights my ability to execute with precision and power during games.
