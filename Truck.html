Quick Overview of the Restoration

  1. Disassembly:
    • Cut off/ remove rusty Bolts
    • Remove body panels
    • Remove mechanics i.e. Engine, transmission, etc...
  2. Restore body panels:
    • Remove old paint and rust with grinder and sandblasting
    • Spray all exposed metal with epoxy primer
    • Bodywork all dents and imperfections with bondo
    • Spray with high build primer, sand smooth and repeat
    • Spray with Primer Sealer
    • Spray with Color Coat
    • Spray with Clear Coat
  3. Reassembly:
    • Put Body panels back on powdercoated frame
    • Hoist engine and install it into the engine bay
    • Rewire and run all fuel lines
    • Install all final trim
    • Fire it up!
