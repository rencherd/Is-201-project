Build Your Table Game

Instructions:

  • Drag the Pieces: Use your mouse to drag the wooden pieces (legs, table top, and flower pot) to the correct positions.
  • Assemble the Table: Position the wooden legs at the bottom corners of the table, and place the table top in the center, touching the legs.
  • Place the Flower Pot: Once the table is assembled, drag the flower pot to the spot directly above the table, and it will automatically snap into place.
  • Complete the Table: Once all the pieces are snapped together, you've built your table with a flower pot on top!

Enjoy building your table!
